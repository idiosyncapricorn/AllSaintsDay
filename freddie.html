Steelo Magazine Vol. 1 “Lost and Found”

I had an amazing time putting the first issue of Steelo together with my friend and fashion killa Robert. This issue is more than just a photoshoot. It is a story of finding yourself in the midst of feeling lost. Being lost or in transition is a scary thing, but we really find who we are when we go through trials and tribulations. We become stronger and clearer after going through storms.

Freddie Nicholson II is a talented designer and photographer hailing from the vibrant Bay Area. His work is a blend of creativity and authenticity, reflecting the diverse and dynamic culture of his surroundings. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for storytelling, Freddie's photography captures moments that resonate with emotion, while his design work pushes boundaries, exploring bold and innovative concepts. Freddie's unique perspective and commitment to his craft have not only garnered attention but have also positioned him as a queer icon in the making. His work is not just about aesthetics; it's about representation and breaking down barriers. As he continues to evolve as an artist, Freddie Nicholson II is set to inspire and influence a new generation of creatives, making waves in both the design and LGBTQ+ communities.
